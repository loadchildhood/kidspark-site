Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: KidSpark games collect no personal data. There are no accounts, no ads, no tracking, and no internet connection required to play. High scores are saved only on your device.

1. Who We Are

KidSpark is an independent game developer creating educational arcade games for children. You can reach us at hello@kidspark.app.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, or any identifying information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device content
  • Payment information

3. What Is Stored on Your Device

The only data stored is your local high scores, saved in your browser's localStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not shared with us or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser/app data

4. Advertising

KidSpark games contain no advertisements of any kind. There are no third-party ad SDKs, no banner ads, no video ads, and no interstitial ads.

5. In-App Purchases

KidSpark games contain no in-app purchases. All games are completely free with no locked features, no premium tiers, and no virtual currencies.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

KidSpark is designed for children and we take this seriously. Our apps are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No accounts or sign-ups are required or offered
  • No social features, chat, or user-generated content
  • No behavioural advertising or data profiling
  • All content is appropriate for children of all ages

7. Internet Permissions

KidSpark games do not require an internet connection to play. The Android app may request internet permission solely to deliver the Progressive Web App shell. No data is sent from your device to our servers.

8. Third-Party Services

KidSpark games do not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, social login providers, or data brokers. The only technology used is standard HTML5 Canvas and Web Audio APIs.

9. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you wish to remove local high scores, clear your app or browser data on your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if our practices change. Any material changes will be reflected with a new "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of KidSpark after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@kidspark.app.
